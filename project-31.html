Remembering Vietnam Exhibition

In November 2017, I completed design and project management work on the Remembering Vietnam Exhibition at the National Archives. I designed the exhibition logo, all interpretive graphics, wall graphics, audio interactive interface, produced video montages, designed the large enivormental graphic timeline, designed the exhibition brochure and completed all digital imaging work for the wall murals. I also managed three separate contracts for design, fabrication and videography for the project. On exhibit November 2017-January 2019. Programs I used: Adobe Illustrator CC, Adobe Photoshop CC, Keynote and CADTools
