Rebranding & Webpage Redesigns, FTC, Division of Consumer & Business Education

My first six months at the FTC were busy with rebranding and redesign projects for legacy website resources and campaigns. The first of these projects, the Weather Emergencies Resource Page, launched for the start of hurricane season. I created a new design with custom illustration work for the webpage graphics, downloadable resource handout and outreach for social media. I provided design work and layout direction for the revamped Drupal 7 webpage. More projects are on the way and will be posted as they launch.


As a visual designer and UX lead on these teams I created wireframes, UX/UI mockups, illustrated infographics, logo design, social media graphics and coordinated the content for design reviews. I used: Adobe Illustrator CC, Adobe InDesign CC, Asana, Google Analytics, Drupal 7
