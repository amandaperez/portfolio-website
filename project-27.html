Amending America Exhibition

In 2016 and 2017, I designed the exhibition logo, all interpretive graphics, infographics, graphic animations and graphic design for the large environmental banner for this exhibiton at the National Archives that features the 27 Amendments to the Consititution and the process of passing an amendment. I also designed a series of graphics for the social media team's promotional campaign. Programs I used: Adobe Illustrator CC, Adobe Photoshop CC, Keynote and CADTools
