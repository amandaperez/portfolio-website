GRAPHIC DESIGN

Programs I Use: Illustrator CC, In Design CC, Photoshop CC, Typekit

  • Designs 2D exhibition graphics (exhibit logos, interpretive graphics, multimedia interfaces, environmental graphics, promotional marketing materials, exterior banners)
  • Develops exhibit color palettes, typefaces, graphic hierarchies and murals for exhibitions
  • Creates color boards, graphic and material specs for all exhibit projects
  • Conceptualizes and creates illustrations, infographics and iconography for exhibition graphics
  • Creates and finalizes all graphic production files based on vendor specifications and proofs final graphics
  • Creates and manages exhibit graphic schedules through each phase of production to meet deadlines and budget requirements
  • Researches content, images and artwork for exhibitions to determine appropriate design solutions within budget
  • Designs and produces project proposal documents and deliverables packages for clients
  • Creates graphic production schedules and cost estimates for fabrication bid process and management
  • Designs print exhibition catalogs, marketing materials and retail merchandising

DIGITAL & UX DESIGN

Programs I Use: HTML, CSS, Sketch, Adobe XD, Various Code Editors, InDesign CC, iBooks Author, After Effects, Keynote, iMovie

  • Nielsen Norman Group UX Certification with Research Focus
  • Visual and UX Design lead on in-house digital strategy, content strategy and analytics teams
  • Designs and develops exhibit prototypes, web wireframes, web mock-ups for design reviews and user testing
  • Facilitates user interviews, user beta testing, card sorting exercises and museum interactive prototyping
  • Conducts analytics research for web projects using Google Analytics, heat mapping, user recordings and A/B testing
  • Creates digital design interfaces for exhibit interactives and produces final production graphics for audio and video exhibit components
  • Designs, develops and publishes downloadable eBooks and ePubs for exhibitions
  • Designs graphic collateral for social media campaigns
  • Builds websites in HTML and CSS

EXHIBIT DESIGN & PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Programs I Use: Vectorworks, CAD Tools

  • Conceptualizes and develops overall look and feel for exhibition spaces incorporating artifacts, audio, video and environmental graphics
  • Produces CAD drawings of floor plans, elevations and interactives for conceptual presentations through fabrication bid packages
  • Creates the project schedule, manages and coordinates the exhibitions team to meet project development milestones and deliverable deadlines
  • Produces biddable fabrication documents for production of 3D exhibition components, graphic panels, interactives and exterior signage
  • Prepares project Scope of Work and RFPs for design, fabrication and usability testing, creates cost estimates and details exhibit fabrication specifications for exhibit bid packages
  • Manages project contracting as a COR for contractor bidding process, graphics production, exhibit fabrication and installation
  • Project art direction from concept to final design and through installation

PHOTOGRAPHY

Programs I Use: Photoshop CC, Lightroom Classic CC, DSLR, iPhone, Moment 18mm, Analog (35mm, 120 (Holga) and Instant Films)

  • Photographer for exhibit graphic content, 3D theatrical structures and exhibits
  • Researches photos for exhibit content and obtains image usage rights for use in exhibits
  • Maintains project photo archive for graphic development and final production
  • Edits project photos and selects all final photos for final graphic layouts
  • Art directs photo shoots for use in exhibit graphics
