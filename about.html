About Me

Analog Hobbies:
Pointed Pen Calligraphy, Hand Lettering, Paper Marbling, Letterpress, Letter Writing, Knitting, Yarn Dying, Travel Photography

EXHIBIT DESIGN

Programs I Use: Vectorworks 2018, CAD Tools

  • Conceptualizes and develops overall look and feel for exhibition spaces incorporating artifacts, audio, video and environmental graphics
  • Produces CAD drawings of floor plans, elevations and interactives for conceptual presentations through fabrication bid packages
  • Creates the project schedule, manages and coordinates the exhibitions team to meet project development milestones and deliverable deadlines
  • Produces biddable fabrication documents for production of 3D exhibition components, graphic panels, interactives and exterior signage
  • Prepares RFPs, writes project Scope of Work and detailed exhibit fabrication specifications for exhibit bid pacakges
  • Manages project contracting as a COR for contractor bidding process, graphics production, exhibit fabrication and installation

GRAPHIC DESIGN

Programs I Use: Illustrator CC, In Design CC, Photoshop CC, Typekit

  • Designs 2D exhibition graphics (exhibit logos, interpretive graphics, multimedia interfaces, enivromental graphics, promotional marketing materials, exterior banners)
  • Develops exhibit color palettes, typefaces, graphic hierarchies and murals for exhibitions
  • Creates color boards, graphic and material specs for all exhibit projects
  • Creates and finalizes all graphic production files based on vendor specifications and proofs final graphics
  • Creates and manages exhibit graphic schedules through each phase of production to meet deadlines and budget requirements
  • Researches content, images and artwork for exhibitions to determine appropriate design solutions within budget
  • Designs and produces project proposal documents and deliverables packages for clients
  • Creates production and graphics schedules and cost estimates for fabrication bid process and management

DIGITAL DESIGN

Programs I Use: HTML, CCS, Sublime Text, Dreamweaver CC, InDesign CC, iBooks Author, After Effects, Keynote, iMovie

  • Creates digital media concepts for 3D and 2D multimedia exhibit interactive components
  • Creates digital media final production graphics for audio and video exhibit stations
  • Designs and develops downloadable eBooks and ePubs for exhibitions
  • Human Centered Design: conducts visitor interviews, card sorting exercises and analytics research for web projects
  • Designs and develops web and mobile mock-ups for agile design reviews
  • Builds websites in HTML and CSS

PHOTOGRAPHY

Programs I Use: Photoshop CC, Bridge, Lightroom, DSLR, iPhone, Analog (35mm, 120 and Instant Films)

  • Photographer for exhibit graphic content, 3D theatrical structures and exhibits
  • Researches photos for exhibit content and obtains image usage rights for use in exhibits
  • Maintains project photo archive for graphic development and final production
  • Edits project photos and selects all final photos for final graphic layouts
  • Art directs photo shoots for use in exhibit graphics
