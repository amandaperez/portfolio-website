Asia Trail Exhibit

I was the lead designer and project art director for this outdoor exhibition at the National Zoo. I designed the exhibit logo, the exhibit graphic look and feel, all the exhibit graphics and hands-on interactives, designed concepts for the multimedia interactives, worked with illustrators and our in-house photographer to create artwork for the project, specified exhibit materials and supervised the installation. Programs I used: Adobe Illustrator, CAD Tools and Adobe Photoshop
