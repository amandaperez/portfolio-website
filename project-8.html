China, 2005

In 2005, I traveled to China with the National Zoo exhibit team on a content development trip for the Asia Trail exhibition. I gathered design research and took hundreds of photos. The design research informed the materials used in the design and the development of interactives in the exhibit. The photos were used throughout the exhibition in the exhibit graphics and as large scale theatrical structures. Programs I used: Adobe Illustrator, CAD Tools, Minolta X700 SLR and Adobe Photoshop
